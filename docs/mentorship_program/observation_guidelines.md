# Classroom observation

1. Presented topics in a logical, well paced sequence? Do you believe a different sequence could benefit learning or give a different perspective?

2. Related lesson to previous material and used tecniques to assess previous knowledge?

3. Do you have similar examples that come from your personal experience? Or do you relate to examples give?

4. Do you believe visuals/handouts were effective ? Can they improve? how?

5. Do you see the theory of this course beeing applyed by the trainers? 
Give one or two examples where you observed it.

6. What parts of the material or approach of the session could be improved? 

7. quality of questions, discussion techniques, students participation. Can any questions or activities be improved? What can be done differently in a _in person_ set up ?

8. List 3 things that went very well in this session.

9. List things that can be improved.

10. Are there points that were not clear for you, or where clarity of the content could be improved?

11. Free reflexions are also encouraged.
